## 2. Kanji Breakdown

### 給 — allowance; supply
- **Reading:** きゅう
- **Radical:** 糸 — meaning "thread / silk"
- **Key Words:** 給料（きゅうりょう）salary、給食（きゅうしょく）school lunch
- **Example:** この求人は給料がいい。*(Kono kyūjin wa kyūryō ga ii.)* — "This job advertisement offers a good salary."

---

### 銀 — silver
- **Reading:** ぎん
- **Radical:** 金 — meaning "money"
- **Memory Hook:** 銀 contains the radical 金 (money) — a bank deals with money.
- **Key Words:** 銀行（ぎんこう）bank
- **Example:** 銀行で手数料をはらう。*(Ginkō de tesūryō wo harau.)* — "I'll pay a commission at the bank."
- **Note:** The correct kanji for "bank" is 銀行 and "commission" is 手数料.

---

### 数 — number
- **Reading:** すう
- **Radical:** 攵 — meaning "to hit"
- **Key Words:** 手数料（てすうりょう）commission
- **Example:** 手数料 — commission (literally: "hand-number-fee")
- **Note:** The radical of 数 is 攵, meaning "to hit".

---

### 送 — to send
- **Reading:** おく-る
- **Radical:** 辶 — meaning "to go forward"
- **Key Words:** 送る（おくる）to send
- **Usage:** 送る can be used for **emails, letters, parcels, and people**.
- **Example:** 友だちにメールを送る。*(Tomodachi ni mēru wo okuru.)* — "I will send an email to a friend."
- **Collocation:** 手紙を送る — to send a letter

---

### 引 — to pull
- **Reading:** ひ-く
- **Radical:** 弓 — meaning "bow (and arrow)"
- **Memory Hook:** 引 looks like a bow being pulled back — 弓 (bow) + a stroke pulling it.
- **Key Words:** 引っこす（ひっこす）to move house
- **Note:** 引っこす is the verb form of 引っ越し（ひっこし）. The radical of 引 is 弓, meaning "bow (and arrow)".
- **Example:** 東京へ引っこす。*(Tōkyō e hikkosu.)* — "I'll move to Tokyo."
- **Collocation:** 家を引っ越す — to move house

## 4. Learning Context

- **Topic:** Kanji for banking, salaries, and moving house
- **Kanji Set:** 給 銀 数 送 引
- **Memory Hooks:**
  - 給 (radical 糸 thread) → supply, like thread being supplied
  - 銀 (radical 金 money) → silver / bank — money connection
  - 数 (radical 攵 to hit) → counting, hitting numbers
  - 送 (radical 辶 go forward) → sending something forward
  - 引 (radical 弓 bow) → pulling back like a bowstring
