## 2. Kanji Spotlight

| Kanji | Reading | Meaning | Radical | Memory Hint |
|-------|---------|---------|---------|-------------|
| 押 | お-す | To press; to push | 扌(hand) | We press things with our hands! |
| 伝 | つた-える | To tell; to inform | 亻(person) | We communicate with other people |
| 消 | き-える | (Lights) go out | 氵(water) | Same radical as 漢（かん） and 泊（と）まる |
| 港 | こう | Port | 氵(water) | Same radical as 海（うみ）sea and 洗（あら）う to wash |
| 号 | ごう | Number | — | Appears in 番号（ばんごう）= number |

### 押 — Common mistake
- **Correct:** 押す *(おす)*
- **Look-alike to avoid:** 狸 *(たぬき)* — raccoon dog. Similar shape but completely different meaning.
- The radical 扌(hand) on the left side is the key to identifying 押.

### 伝 — Two words from one kanji

The kanji 伝 appears in two related words:

| Japanese | Romaji | Meaning |
|----------|--------|---------|
| 伝える | つたえる | To tell; to inform (transitive — you tell someone something) |
| 手伝う | てつだう | To help (lit. "lend a hand to transmit/pass along") |

- **Example:** 友だちに時間を伝える。*(Tomodachi ni jikan wo tsutaeru.)* — "Tell a friend the time."
- **Example:** お母さんの料理を手伝う。*(Okaasan no ryoori wo tetsudau.)* — "Help mom with cooking."

## 4. Pronunciation Note — Long vowels

- 空港 reads **くうこう** (kuukou) — not くこう
- う lengthens the "o" sound of any hiragana ending in "o"
- 番号 reads **ばんごう** (bangou) — the う after ご lengthens the "o"
