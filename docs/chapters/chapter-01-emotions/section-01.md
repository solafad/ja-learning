# Sharing feelings and the reasons for them

## 1. Vocabulary List

| Japanese | Romaji   | English                          |
|----------|----------|----------------------------------|
| うれしい  | ureshii  | Glad / Happy (situational)       |
| かなしい  | kanashii | Sad                              |
| しあわせ  | shiawase | Happy (long-term / well-being)   |
| なぜなら  | nazenara | Because / The reason is          |

---

## 2. Grammar & Sentences

**Expressing Reasons:** Linking an emotional state to a cause.

- **Grammar Rule:** `[Reason/Event]` + から (kara) + `[Emotion]`
- **Sentence Structure:** なぜなら、[Reason] 〜からです。
- **Key Phrase:** 私はとてもうれしいです。*(Watashi wa totemo ureshii desu.)* — "I am very happy."

**Using Modifiers:**
- ちょっと — *a little*
- とても — *very*

Use these to adjust the weight of the emotion.

---

## 3. Learning Context

- **Word Nuance:** うれしい = joy from an immediate event vs. しあわせ = a deeper, lasting sense of happiness.
- **Sentence Logic:** In intermediate Japanese, providing the reason (なぜなら) before the result is a key step toward fluency.
- **Course Context:** Chapter 1 of the *Complete Japanese Intermediate B1* course on Busuu.
