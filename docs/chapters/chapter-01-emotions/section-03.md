# Expressing frustration and anger

## 1. Vocabulary List

| Japanese   | Romaji      | English                            |
|------------|-------------|------------------------------------|
| くやしい    | kuyashii    | Frustrated / Vexed                 |
| おこる      | okoru       | To get angry                       |
| けんかする  | kenka suru  | To have an argument / fight        |
| 彼氏        | kareshi     | Boyfriend                          |
| 先週        | senshuu     | Last week                          |

---

## 2. Grammar & Sentences

**Past Tense of i-Adjectives:** To express a past emotion, change the final 〜い to 〜かったです.

- **Structure:** `[Adjective base]` + かったです
- **Key Phrase:** くやしかったです。*(Kuyashikatta desu.)* — "I was frustrated."

**Verbal Sentences (Past Tense):** Use the 〜ました form for completed actions.

- **Example:** 友だちはおこりました。*(Tomodachi wa okorimashita.)* — "My friend got angry."

**Time Markers:** Use 先週 (senshuu) to set the context for past events.

- **Example:** 先週、彼氏とけんかした。*(Senshuu, kareshi to kenka shita.)* — "Last week, I had an argument with my boyfriend."
