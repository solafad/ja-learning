## 2. Grammar & Sentences

### Three-Way Review

| Structure | Direction | Meaning |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| て form ＋ **あげる** | speaker → someone else | to do something for others |
| て form ＋ **くれる** | someone else → speaker | to receive a favour from someone |
| て form ＋ **もらう** | speaker receives (via request) | to get someone to do something for you |

---

### て Form ＋ もらう — Getting Someone to Do a Favour

**Meaning:** "I get [person] to do [action] for me." / "I have [person] do [action]."  
**Structure:** `receiver は` + `giver に` + `(object を)` + `verb て form` + `もらいます`

**Key sentence:**
- 私は父にスマホを**買ってもらいます**。*(Watashi wa chichi ni sumaho wo katte moraimasu.)* — "I'll get my dad to buy me a smartphone."

---

### Particle Focus: は and に

In a てもらう sentence:
- The person **after は** = the one **receiving** the favour (almost always the speaker)
- The person **after に** = the one **performing** the action

**Example breakdown:**

| 私 **は** | 友だち **に** | 宿題 **を** | 手伝って | もらいました。|
|-----------|------------|-----------|---------|------------|
| I (receiver) | friend (doer) **に** | homework | help | received the favour |

→ 私は友だちに宿題を手伝ってもらいました。*(Watashi wa tomodachi ni shukudai wo tetsudatte moraimashita.)* — "I got my friend to help me with my homework."

---

### てもらう — Example Sentences

| Japanese | Romaji | English |
|----------|--------|---------|
| 私は父にスマホを買ってもらいました。 | Watashi wa chichi ni sumaho wo katte moraimashita. | I got my dad to buy me a smartphone. |
| 私は友だちに宿題を手伝ってもらいました。 | Watashi wa tomodachi ni shukudai wo tetsudatte moraimashita. | I got my friend to help me with my homework. |
| 私は店の人にこわれたスマホをなおしてもらいました。 | Watashi wa mise no hito ni kowareta sumaho wo naoshite moraimashita. | I got the person in the shop to fix the broken smartphone. |

### てあげる in context (from the same lesson)

- 私は入国カードを書いたとき、となりの座席の人にペンを貸してあげました。*(Watashi wa nyuukoku kaado wo kaita toki, tonari no zaseki no hito ni pen wo kashite agemashita.)* — "When I had filled in the arrival card, I lent a pen to the person in the seat next to me."

---

### もらう vs くれる — Same Meaning, Different Topic

Both sentences below mean the same thing, but the **topic (は)** is different:

| Japanese | English | Topic |
|----------|---------|-------|
| **私は**父にスマホを買ってもらいます。 | I'll get my dad to buy me a smartphone. | 私は — speaker is topic |
| **父は**私にスマホを買ってくれます。 | My dad will buy me a smartphone. | 父は — dad is topic |

> **Key insight:** もらう focuses on the receiver (speaker) getting something. くれる focuses on the giver doing something for the speaker. The underlying event is the same — only the perspective shifts.

---

### くれる in context (from the same lesson)

- 私の便は遅れました。友だちは一時間私を待ってくれました。*(Watashi no bin wa okuremashita. Tomodachi wa ichijikan watashi wo matte kuremashita.)* — "My flight was delayed. My friend waited for me for an hour."

---

### 〜方（かた）— "How to / Way of doing"

Attach **方** to the **verb stem** to mean "the way of doing X" or "how to do X."

| Verb | 〜方 Form | Meaning |
|------|----------|---------|
| 行く | 行き方（いきかた） | way to go / directions |
| 買う | 買い方（かいかた） | how to buy |
| 食べる | 食べ方（たべかた） | how to eat |
| 作る | 作り方（つくりかた） | how to make |

**Examples from this lesson:**
- 駅からホテルまでの**行き方**を教えてもらいました。*(directions from station to hotel)*
- 切ぶの**買い方**を教えてもらいました。*(how to buy a ticket)*

## 3. Learning Context

- **Grammar Focus:** て form ＋ もらう — "getting someone to do something for you." Completes the あげる / くれる / もらう triad for actions.
- **Structure:** 私は + [person]に + [action]てもらう — receiver は, doer に.
- **もらう vs くれる:** Same event, different topic — もらう highlights the receiver's perspective, くれる highlights the giver's perspective.
- **New Pattern:** 〜方（かた） = "how to / way of doing" — attach to verb stem. Very productive pattern used in natural Japanese (行き方, 買い方, etc.).
- **Context:** Arriving in Japan — getting help from strangers, shop staff, family, and cabin crew.
- **Vocabulary Note:** 座席（ざせき）= seat (formal, e.g. on a plane/train); 座（すわ）る = to sit.
