## 2. Grammar & Sentences

**ば Form — Hopes and Expectations:** Use the ば form to express "if" conditions with a positive or expected outcome.

- **Structure:** Verb ば form + result clause
- **Key Phrase / Example:** 日本へ行けば、日本語が上手になります。*(Nihon e ikeba, nihongo ga jouzu ni narimasu.)* — "If you go to Japan, you'll become good at Japanese."
- **Example:** ここを読めば、分かります。*(Koko o yomeba, wakarimasu.)* — "If you read here, you'll understand it."

**〜ば、よかった — Expressing Regret (idiomatic):** Literally "if [I had done something], it would have been better." The tense at the end signals past regret vs. future hope.

- **Structure:** Verb ば form + `よかったです` (past regret) / `いいです` (future hope)
- **Regret:** もっと勉強すれば、よかったです。*(Motto benkyou sureba, yokatta desu.)* — "I wish I had studied more."
- **Hope:** もっと勉強すれば、いいです。*(Motto benkyou sureba, ii desu.)* — "If I study more, I'll be ok."

**Positive vs. Negative ば Form:**

- **Structure:** 持（も）っていれば (if I have) / 持（も）っていなければ (if I don't have)
- **Example:** 日本のパスポートを持っていなければ、ここで待ってください。*(Nihon no pasupooto o motte inakereba, koko de matte kudasai.)* — "If you don't have a Japanese passport, please wait here."

**Story Context — 空港（くうこう）から (From the Airport):**

- はら宿（じゅく）駅（えき）で電車（でんしゃ）を降（お）りれば、ホテルにちかいです。*(Harajuku-eki de densha o orireba, hoteru ni chikai desu.)* — "If he gets off the train at Harajuku station, it's close to the hotel."
- 急（いそ）げば、あの電車に乗（の）れます。*(Isogeba, ano densha ni noremasu.)* — "If I hurry, I can get on that train."
- この電車に間（ま）に合（あ）えば、八時（はちじ）までにホテルに着（つ）きます。*(Kono densha ni maniaeba, hachiji made ni hoteru ni tsukimasu.)* — "If I'm in time for this train, I'll arrive at the hotel by 8."
- 駅（えき）に行（い）けば、電車（でんしゃ）の時間（じかん）が分（わ）かります。*(Eki ni ikeba, densha no jikan ga wakarimasu.)* — "If I go to the station, I can find out the train times."

## 3. Learning Context

- **Lesson Title:** よく休んだ？(Did you rest well?)
- **Focus:** ば form for expressing hopes, expectations, and regrets in travel contexts
- **Key Conjugations:** 行（い）く→行けば、読（よ）む→読めば、聞（き）く→聞けば、急（いそ）ぐ→急げば、降（お）りる→降りれば、間（ま）に合（あ）う→間に合えば
- **Idiomatic Note:** 〜ば、よかった signals regret about the past; 〜ば、いいです signals a positive future condition
