## 2. Kanji Breakdown

### 代 — charge / cost / price
- **Reading:** だい
- **Radical:** イ — meaning "person"
- **Memory Hook:** 仕 and 伝 share the same radical イ.
- **Key Words:** 代金（だいきん）price, ガス代 gas bill, 電話代 phone bill, 電気代 electricity bill
- **Culture Note:** 代金 refers specifically to the money a buyer pays to a seller — it is more formal than just 金.
- **Example:** 洋服の代金をはらいます。*(Yōfuku no daikin wo haraimasu.)* — "I'll pay for the clothes."

---

### 費 — cost / expense
- **Reading:** ひ
- **Radical:** 貝 — meaning "shell"
- **Memory Hook:** 買 shares the same radical 貝. The radical 貝 is often used for money-related kanji because shells were used as money long ago.
- **Key Words:** 消費ぜい（しょうひぜい）consumption tax
- **Example:** しょうひぜいがかかります。*(Shōhizei ga kakarimasu.)* — "Consumption tax will be charged."

---

### 越 — to cross over; to move (house)
- **Reading:** こ-す
- **Radical:** 走 — meaning "to run"
- **Memory Hook:** 走 and 起 share the same radical.
- **Key Compounds:**

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 引っ越す | ひっこす | to move (house) — verb |
| 引っ越し | ひっこし | move (house) — noun |
| 引っ越し屋 | ひっこしや | moving company |

> **Grammar Note:** 引っ越し is a **noun** (the act of moving); 引っ越す is a **verb** (to move). The noun is formed by dropping ます from 引っ越します → 引っ越し.

- **Example:** 引っ越すので、不動産屋に行きます。*(Hikkosu node, fudōsan-ya ni ikimasu.)* — "Because I'm moving house, I'll go to an estate agency."
- **Example:** さらさんは引っ越しのじゅんびをします。*(Sara-san wa hikkoshi no junbi wo shimasu.)* — "Sara is preparing to move house."

---

### 産 — products; to give birth
- **Reading:** さん
- **Radical:** 生 — meaning "life"
- **Memory Hook:** 産 originally came from 彦 (man) and 生 (birth) — it meant "the birth of a virtuous person". The radical 生 is also in words like 生まれる (to be born).
- **Key Compounds:**

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 不動産 | ふどうさん | property / real estate |
| 不動産屋 | ふどうさんや | estate agency |
| 日本産 | にほんさん | made in Japan |
| 産地 | さんち | producing area |

- **Pattern:** `[Place name]` + 産 → describes **where a product comes from**

| Japanese | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| 北海道産のやさい | vegetables grown in Hokkaido |
| 青もり産のりんご | apples grown in Aomori |
| 秋た産のこめ | rice grown in Akita |
| しずおか産のお茶 | green tea produced in Shizuoka |

- **Example:** 不動産屋に行ってアパートをさがします。*(Fudōsan-ya ni itte apāto wo sagashimasu.)* — "I will go to an estate agency and look for an apartment."

---

### 窓 — window
- **Reading:** まど
- **Radical:** 穴 — meaning "cave"
- **Memory Hook:** The top half looks like katakana ウ, with ハ and ム below it. The bottom part is 心, meaning "heart".
- **Key Words:** 窓を開ける (to open a window), 窓を閉める (to close a window)
- **Example:** 窓を開ける。*(Mado wo akeru.)* — "To open a window."
- **Example:** 雨が降っているから窓を閉めてください。*(Ame ga futte iru kara mado wo shimete kudasai.)* — "Please close the window because it is raining."

## 5. Learning Context

- **Topic:** Kanji for housing costs, moving, property, and home features — 代 費 越 産 窓
- **Kanji Set:** 代 費 越 産 窓
- **Memory Hooks:**
  - 代（イ person）— a person paying a charge; shared with 仕, 伝
  - 費（貝 shell）— shells = money → cost; shared with 買
  - 越（走 to run）— running across / crossing over; shared with 起
  - 産（生 life）— birth → products of the earth; shared with 生まれる
  - 窓（穴 cave）— a cave opening = a window; heart (心) at the bottom
- **Grammar Notes:**
  - `[Place]` + 産 → origin label (日本産, 北海道産)
  - 引っ越し (noun) vs 引っ越す (verb) — drop ます from verb form to get noun
  - 代 as suffix: utility name + 代 = that utility's bill
