## 2. Kanji Breakdown

### 注 — to concentrate on; to pour
- **Reading:** ちゅう
- **Radical:** 氵 — meaning "water"
- **Memory Hook:** The right part is 主, which depicts a burning candle — a flame at the **centre**. Pouring water with focus = concentrating.
- **Key Compounds:**

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 注意 | ちゅうい | caution; attention |
| 注意する | ちゅういする | to be careful |
| 注文 | ちゅうもん | order (food/goods) |
| 注文する | ちゅうもんする | to order |

> **Culture Note:** 注文 originally meant "a document describing what was needed." Today it simply means placing an order at a restaurant or shop.

- **Example:** 飛び出し注意。*(Tobidashi chūi.)* — "Watch out — children jumping out." *(common street sign)*
- **Example:** 注意してつつみます。*(Chūi shite tsutsumimasu.)* — "I'll wrap it carefully."
- **Example:** アイスコーヒーを注文する。*(Aisu kōhī wo chūmon suru.)* — "I'll order an iced coffee."

---

### 相 — mutual; minister
- **Reading:** そう
- **Radical:** 目 — meaning "eye"
- **Memory Hook:** When we consult someone, we look into their **eyes** (目). The left part 木 (tree) + eye (目) = observing carefully together.
- **Key Compounds:**

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 相談する | そうだんする | to consult; to ask for advice |
| 相談に乗る | そうだんにのる | to give advice |

- **Example:** 日本の大学に行きたいので、先生に相談します。*(Nihon no daigaku ni ikitai node, sensei ni sōdan shimasu.)* — "Because I want to go to university in Japan, I will consult my teacher."

---

### 談 — to discuss
- **Reading:** だん
- **Radical:** 言 — meaning "speech"
- **Memory Hook:** 言 (speech/words) is shared by 話, 読, 語 — all language-related kanji. 談 = using words to discuss together.
- **Key Compounds:**

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 相談する | そうだんする | to consult; to ask for advice |
| 相談に乗る | そうだんにのる | to give advice |

- **Example:** 友達のことを心配しているんだったら、相談してください。*(Tomodachi no koto wo shinpai shite iru n dattara, sōdan shite kudasai.)* — "If you are worrying about your friends, please ask me for advice."

---

### 心 — heart; mind
- **Reading:** しん (on-reading); こころ (kun-reading)
- **Radical:** 心 — 心 itself is a radical
- **Memory Hook:** Many emotion kanji use 心 — 思（おも・う）to think, 急（きゅう）urgent, 怒（おこ・る）angry, 悲（かな・しい）sad, 恥（は・ずかしい）embarrassed. The heart drives all emotions.
- **Key Compounds:**

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 心配する | しんぱいする | to worry |
| 安心 | あんしん | relief; peace of mind |
| 心 | こころ | heart; mind |
| 中心 | ちゅうしん | centre; core |

- **Example:** まちの中心にとしょかんがあります。*(Machi no chūshin ni toshokan ga arimasu.)* — "There is a library in the centre of the town."
- **Example:** 引っ越しはお金がかかるので、心配しています。*(Hikkoshi wa okane ga kakaru node, shinpai shite imasu.)* — "Because moving house costs money, I'm worried."

---

### 配 — to distribute; to worry
- **Reading:** はい; ぱい (in compounds)
- **Radical:** 酉 — meaning "rice wine; sake jar"
- **Memory Hook:** 酉 (sake jar) = distributing drinks at a party. When you 心配 (worry), your heart (心) is being distributed in all directions with anxiety.
- **Key Compounds:**

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 心配する | しんぱいする | to worry |

- **Example:** 引っ越しはお金がかかるので、心配しています。*(Hikkoshi wa okane ga kakaru node, shinpai shite imasu.)* — "Because moving house costs money, I'm worried."

## 5. Learning Context

- **Topic:** Kanji for advice, attention, worry, and the heart — 注 相 談 心 配
- **Kanji Set:** 注 相 談 心 配
- **Memory Hooks:**
  - 注（氵 water）— pour water with focus = concentrate; 主 (candle) suggests the centre
  - 相（目 eye）— looking into someone's eyes = consulting together
  - 談（言 speech）— shared with 話, 読, 語; words used in discussion
  - 心（心 heart）— heart drives all emotions; appears in 思, 急, 怒, 悲, 恥
  - 配（酉 sake jar）— distributing drinks → distributing worry = 心配
- **Grammar Notes:**
  - 相談する = to seek advice (you go to someone); 相談に乗る = to give advice (someone comes to you)
  - 心配する is a suru-verb compound: 心配 (noun) + する
