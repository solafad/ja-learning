## 2. Grammar & Sentences

### から — "Because" (reason, spoken/casual)

Used to explain the reason for an action in a natural, conversational way.

- **Structure:** `[Reason clause]` + から、`[Result clause]`
- **Key Phrase:** 引っこすから、じゅんびをします。*(Hikkosu kara, junbi o shimasu.)* — "Because I'm moving house, I'm making preparations."

---

### て form + おきます — "Do in advance"

Used when performing an action **ahead of time** in preparation for something else. Review from Chapter 5.

- **Structure:** `[Verb て-form]` + おきます
- **Key Phrase:** 本とCDをたなから出しておきます。*(Hon to CD o tana kara dashite okimasu.)* — "I take the books and CDs off the shelf (in advance)."

| Japanese | Romaji | English |
|----------|--------|---------|
| おさらをはこに入れて、ガムテープでとめます。 | Osara o hako ni irete, gamu tēpu de tomemasu. | We put plates in the box and secure it with parcel tape. |
| ガムテープでとめます。 | Gamu tēpu de tomemasu. | I secure it with parcel tape. |
| ここをガムテープでとめてください。 | Koko o gamu tēpu de tomete kudasai. | Please secure here with parcel tape. |

> **Note:** The て-form of **とめる** is **とめて**. The て-form of **入れる** is **入れて**.

---

### とき — "When"

Used to describe the timing of an action or a condition.

- **Structure:** `[Verb dictionary form / Noun + の]` + とき
- **Key Phrase:** はさみを使うとき、ちゅう意してください。*(Hasami o tsukau toki, chūi shite kudasai.)* — "Please be careful when you use scissors."

---

### ので + てください — "Because…, please do…"

Combines a reason with a polite request.

- **Key Phrase:** これは大切なので、ふろしきでつつんでください。*(Kore wa taisetsu na node, furoshiki de tsutsunde kudasai.)* — "Because this is very important, please wrap it with furoshiki."

---

## 3. Learning Context

- **Topic:** Vocabulary and expressions for packing when moving house
- **Key Structures:** から (reason), て-form + おきます (action in advance), とき (when), ので + てください (reason + request)
- **Cultural Note:** **ふろしき（風呂敷）** is a traditional Japanese wrapping cloth used to wrap and transport items. Wrapping style varies by the shape and weight of contents. Colours also vary by occasion — red furoshiki is used for celebrations.
