## 2. Kanji Breakdown

### 近 — near / close
- **Readings:** ちか-い (adjective) / きん (compound prefix)
- **Radical:** 辶 — meaning "to go forward"
- **Memory Hook:** The same radical 辶 is used in 送 (to send) and 道 (road) — all involve moving forward.
- **Key Words:** 近い (near), 近所（きんじょ）(neighbourhood)
- **Culture Note:** 近所の人 (*kinjō no hito*) means "neighbours".
- **Example:** この近くにペット可のカフェはありますか。*(Kono chikaku ni petto ka no kafe wa arimasu ka.)* — "Are there any cafes where pets are allowed near here?"

---

### 利 — advantage
- **Reading:** り
- **Radical:** 刂 — meaning "sword"
- **Memory Hook:** 前 (*mae*, before/front) shares the same radical 刂.
- **Key Words:** 便利（べんり）convenient
- **Example:** 家の近くに新しい駅ができて、便利になった。*(Ie no chikaku ni atarashii eki ga dekite, benri ni natta.)* — "A new station was built near my house, and it became more convenient."
- **Grammar Note:** 便利な is a **na-adjective**. To use it with なる (to become), drop the な and add に → 便利になる.

---

### 可 — allowed
- **Reading:** か
- **Radical:** 口 — meaning "mouth"
- **Key Words:** ペット可（か）(pets allowed), 不可（ふか）(not allowed / inadvisable)
- **Example:** この近くにペット可のカフェはありますか。*(Kono chikaku ni petto ka no kafe wa arimasu ka.)* — "Are there any cafes where pets are allowed near here?"
- **Reading Drill:** ペット可 → ペットか

---

### 暗 — dark
- **Reading:** くら-い
- **Radical:** 日 — meaning "sun"
- **Memory Hook:** 暗 contains 日 (sun) — when there is no sun, it is dark. 明（あかるい）, 時（とき）, and 晩（ばん）share the same radical.
- **Key Words:** 暗い（くらい）dark
- **Grammar Note:** 暗い is an **i-adjective** — it can connect directly to nouns without changing form: 暗い道 (the dark road).
- **Example:** まやさんは暗い道を歩きません。*(Maya-san wa kurai michi wo arukimasen.)* — "Maya doesn't walk on the dark street."
- **Example:** 暗くなると、よく見えなくて不便です。*(Kuraku naru to, yoku mienakute fuben desu.)* — "When it's dark, I can't see well and it's inconvenient."

---

### 不 — negative prefix
- **Reading:** ふ
- **Radical:** 一 — meaning "one"
- **Memory Hook:** 上（うえ）and 下（した）share the same radical 一.
- **Function:** 不 is **added at the beginning** of a word to make it negative (like "un-" or "in-" in English).
- **Key Compounds:**

| Kanji | Reading | Meaning |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 不便 | ふべん | inconvenient |
| 不可 | ふか | inadvisable / not allowed |
| 不幸 | ふこう | unhappiness |
| 不安 | ふあん | insecurity / anxiety |

- **Formula:** 不（negative）＋ 便（convenient）＝ 不便（inconvenient）
- **Example:** 車がこわれたので、不便です。*(Kuruma ga kowareta node, fuben desu.)* — "Because my car is broken, it is inconvenient."
- **Example:** 暗くなると、よく見えなくて不便です。*(Kuraku naru to, yoku mienakute fuben desu.)* — "When it's dark, I can't see well and it's inconvenient."

## 6. Learning Context

- **Topic:** Kanji for neighbourhoods and convenience — 近 利 可 暗 不
- **Kanji Set:** 近 利 可 暗 不
- **Memory Hooks:**
  - 近（辶 go forward）— moving close to something
  - 利（刂 sword）— same as 前; an "advantage" cuts through
  - 可（口 mouth）— a mouth saying "yes, allowed"
  - 暗（日 sun）— no sun = dark; shares radical with 明, 時, 晩
  - 不（一 one）— a line negating everything above it; "un-" prefix in Japanese
- **Grammar Notes:**
  - 便利な + なる → drop な, add に: **便利になる** (become convenient)
  - 暗い is an i-adjective → connects directly to nouns: **暗い道**
  - 不 is a productive negative prefix: 不便, 不可, 不幸, 不安
