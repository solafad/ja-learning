## 2. Grammar & Sentences

**Placement of Onomatopoeia:** These words act as adverbs and appear right before the verb.

**The "State" Verb:** Many of these words pair with する to describe a feeling.

- **Example:** 緊張してどきどきします。*(Kinchou shite dokidoki shimasu.)* — "I'm nervous and my heart is pounding."

**Fluent Progression:** Combining どんどん and ぺらぺら to describe a goal.

- **Example:** 日本語がどんどんぺらぺらになります。*(Nihongo ga dondon perapera ni narimasu.)* — "I will steadily become fluent in Japanese."
