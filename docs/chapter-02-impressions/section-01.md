---

## 2. Grammar & Sentences

**Kanji Integration:** Transitioning from hiragana-only emotional descriptors to their standard kanji forms.

**Verbal vs. Adjectival States:**

- 怒る・泣く are **verbs** — they describe actions or ongoing states
- 悲しい・恥ずかしい are **i-adjectives** — they describe feelings directly

**Connecting adjective + verb:** Use the て-form of an i-adjective (〜くて) to chain two descriptions.

- **Key Sentence:** 彼は悲しくて泣いています。*(Kare wa kanashikute naite imasu.)* — "He is sad and crying."
