## 2. Grammar & Sentences

### どのくらい / どのぐらい — How Long / How Much / How Difficult

Both forms are interchangeable. Use at the start of a question to ask about duration, amount, or degree.

**Duration:**
- どのくらい日本語を勉強していますか。*(Dono kurai nihongo wo benkyou shite imasu ka.)* — How long have you been learning Japanese?
- どのくらいすもうのけいけんがありますか。*(Dono kurai sumou no keiken ga arimasu ka.)* — How long have you had sumo experience?

**Amount / Degree:**
- どのぐらい食べますか。*(Dono gurai tabemasu ka.)* — How much do you eat?
- どのくらいむずかしいですか。*(Dono kurai muzukashii desu ka.)* — How difficult is it?
- どのくらいさむいですか。*(Dono kurai samui desu ka.)* — How cold is it?
- よこづなになるのは、どのくらいむずかしいですか。*(Yokozuna ni naru no wa, dono kurai muzukashii desu ka.)* — How difficult is it to become a yokozuna?

### やく / 約 — Approximately

Place やく before a number + time unit. It signals the figure is not exact.

- 私はやく四か月間日本語を勉強しています。*(Watashi wa yaku yonkagetsu-kan nihongo wo benkyou shite imasu.)* — I've been studying Japanese for approximately 4 months.
- やく三週間日本に行きます。*(Yaku san-shuukan nihon ni ikimasu.)* — I will go to Japan for approximately 3 weeks.
- やく五年間アメリカに住んでいました。*(Yaku go-nenkan Amerika ni sunde imashita.)* — I was living in the US for approximately 5 years.
- やく十日後に東京に行きます。*(Yaku tooka-go ni Tokyo ni ikimasu.)* — In approximately 10 days, I will go to Tokyo.

Note: 後（ご）= "after" when counting forward from now. 後（あと）= "later", 後（うしろ）= "behind".

### けいけん + ある — Expressing Experience

ある does not only mean "there is" — it is also used to express possession, including experience.

**Pattern:** `[activity description] + けいけんはありますか`

- 書道のけいけんはありますか。*(Shodou no keiken wa arimasu ka.)* — Do you have any calligraphy experience?
- りゅう学のけいけんはありますか。*(Ryuugaku no keiken wa arimasu ka.)* — Do you have any experience studying abroad?
- 日本で仕事をしたけいけんはありますか。*(Nihon de shigoto wo shita keiken wa arimasu ka.)* — Do you have any experience working in Japan?
- おすもうさんに会ったけいけんがあります。*(Osumou-san ni atta keiken ga arimasu.)* — I have experienced meeting a sumo wrestler.

**Key pattern:** To modify けいけん, place the description (verb in plain past form) *before* the noun:
`日本で仕事をした` + `けいけん` = "experience of working in Japan"

## 3. Learning Context

- **どのくらい vs どのぐらい:** Fully interchangeable — choose either freely.
- **やく precision:** やく signals approximation; the sentence is False if it claims the figure is exact.
- **Noun modification order:** Japanese modifiers precede nouns — `おすもうさんに会った + けいけん` follows the same rule as adjectives before nouns.
- **Sumo dialogue:** Interview with a yokozuna who has ~16 years of experience since age 5, eats a lot, is never cold (always moving), and says reaching yokozuna rank is extremely difficult.
