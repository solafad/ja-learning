## 3. Kanji Breakdown — 飛行機

飛行機 is built from three kanji, each with its own meaning and reading:

| Kanji | Reading in 飛行機 | Meaning |
|-------|-----------------|---------|
| 飛 | ひ | To fly |
| 行 | こう | Go / travel (note: 行 is usually read as い-く, but here reads こう) |
| 機 | き | Machine |

> Most kanji have multiple readings! **行** is read as **こう** in 飛行機, but we often use it as **行（い）く**.

---

## 4. 便 — Two meanings

The kanji 便（びん）has two main uses:

| Word | Reading | Meaning |
|------|---------|---------|
| 〇〇便 | びん | Flight number / service (e.g. 16便 = flight 16) |
| ゆう便きょく | ゆうびんきょく | Post office (ゆう便きょく is a place offering postal services) |

- **Confusable kanji:** 便 looks similar to **使（つかう）** — both share the radical **亻**

---

## 5. 席 — Radical 广

The radical of 席 is **广**, meaning "building." Other kanji with the same radical:

| Kanji | Reading | Meaning |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 座 | すわる | To sit |
| 店 | みせ | Shop / store |
| 席 | せき | Seat |

## 6. 何 ＋ Counter — Asking about numbers

何 is used with a counter to ask "how many ~":

| Japanese | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 何便 | なんびん | How many flights |
| 何人 | なんにん | How many people |
| 何匹 | なんびき | How many animals / insects |
| 何枚 | なんまい | How many flat objects |
| 何本 | なんぼん | How many long objects |
| 何台 | なんだい | How many large furniture / electrical items |
