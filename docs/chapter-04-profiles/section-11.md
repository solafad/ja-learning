## 2. Listening Story — Jun's Birthday Cake

**Context:** Jun wants to make a birthday cake for his father.

**Full dialogue (English):**

- Jun: Mom, tomorrow is Dad's birthday, isn't it? I want to make a cake.
- Mom: That's a good idea. Your dad will be glad and he might cry.
- Jun: What size would be good?
- Mom: Let me see, I think this size would be good because we are a family of 4. It'd be a good idea to look at this recipe when you make it.
- Jun: This cake looks delicious. Do you think Dad would like this cake?
- Mom: Yes, because your dad likes fruit a lot.
- Jun: OK, let's wash our hands and make the cake!

**Full dialogue (Japanese):**

- じゅん：お母さん、明日はお父さんのたん生日だよね。ぼく、ケーキを作りたいんだ。
- お母さん：それはいいね。お父さんはうれしくて、なくかも。
- じゅん：どの大きさがいい？
- お母さん：そうね、うちは４人家族だからこれがいいと思う。この本を見ながら作ったらいいよ。
- じゅん：このケーキはおいしそう。お父さんはこのケーキが好きだと思う？
- お母さん：うん、お父さんはくだものが大好きだから。
- じゅん：じゃあ手を洗ってケーキを作ろう。

## 3. Grammar & Sentences

**〜ながら — Doing two things simultaneously:** Attach ながら to the stem of a verb to say you do two actions at the same time.

- **Structure:** `Verb stem` + `ながら` + main action
- **Example:** テレビを見ながら料理する。*(Terebi wo minagara ryoori suru.)* — "I watch TV while I cook."
- **From story:** この本を見ながら作ったらいいよ。*(Kono hon wo minagara tsukuttara ii yo.)* — "It'd be a good idea to look at this recipe when you make it."

**〜たら — Conditional / When:** Used to describe what to do when something happens.

- **Structure:** `Verb (past form)` + `ら`
- **Example:** この本を見ながら作ったらいいよ。*(Kono hon wo minagara tsukuttara ii yo.)* — "It'd be a good idea to look at this while making it."

**〜かも (しれない) — Might / Maybe:**

- **Structure:** `Plain form` + `かも`
- **Example:** お父さんはうれしくて、なくかも。*(Otōsan wa ureshikute, naku kamo.)* — "Your dad will be glad and he might cry."

**〜だから — Because:**

- **Structure:** `Reason` + `だから`
- **Example:** お父さんはくだものが大好きだから。*(Otōsan wa kudamono ga daisuki dakara.)* — "Because your dad likes fruit a lot."

## 4. Learning Context

- **Lesson:** Listening to a birthday cake discussion
- **Type:** Listening comprehension (review)
- **Story:** Jun plans to make a cake for his father's birthday; his mother advises him on size and recommends looking at a recipe book.
- **Key comprehension facts:**
  - Jun will make a cake (not buy one) for his father's birthday
  - Jun's father will be glad (うれしい) and happy (幸せ) — not sad
  - Jun's mother suggested looking at a recipe book
  - Jun's father likes fruit
