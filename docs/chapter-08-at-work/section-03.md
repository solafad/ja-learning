## 2. Kanji Breakdown

### 正 — correct / right
- **Reading:** ただしい (adjective) / せい (prefix in compounds)
- **Radical:** 止 — meaning "to stop"
- **Key Words:** 正しい (correct), 正社員 (permanent employee)
- **Example:** 正しいこたえはどれですか。*(Tadashii kotae wa dore desu ka.)* — "Which is the correct answer?"

---

### 員 — member
- **Reading:** いん
- **Radical:** 口 — meaning "mouth"
- **Key Words:** 店員 (shop assistant), 正社員 (permanent employee)
- **Example:** 店員（てんいん）/ 正社員（せいしゃいん）— shop assistant / permanent employee

---

### 職 — employment; work
- **Reading:** しょく
- **Radical:** 耳 — meaning "ear"
- **Key Words:** 就職する (to start a job), 職場 (workplace)
- **Example:** 職場の友だちと出かける。*(Shokuba no tomodachi to dekakeru.)* — "I go out with friends from work."

---

### 面 — face; surface
- **Reading:** めん
- **Radical:** 面 is itself a radical, meaning "face" or "surface"
- **Key Words:** 面接 (interview)
- **Example:** 面接（めんせつ）— interview

---

### 求 — to request; to demand
- **Reading:** きゅう (noun compounds) / もとめる (verb)
- **Radical:** 氷 — meaning "water"
- **Key Words:** 求人 (job advertisement), 求める (to request)
- **Example:** 求人を見て面せつに来ました。*(Kyūjin wo mite mensetsu ni kimashita.)* — "I saw a job advertisement and came for an interview."

**Reading drill — correct readings:**
- 正社員 → せいしゃいん
- 面接 → めんせつ
- 求人 → きゅうじん

**Phrase examples:**
- 正社員の仕事の面せつ。*(Seishain no shigoto no mensetsu.)* — "An interview for a permanent employee position."
- 電話会社の求人。*(Denwa gaisha no kyūjin.)* — "A job advertisement at a telephone company."

## 4. Learning Context

- **Topic:** Kanji for workplace and job-hunting vocabulary
- **Kanji Set:** 正 員 職 面 求
- **Memory Hooks:** 正's radical is 止 (stop) — think of stopping to check if something is *correct*. 員's radical is 口 (mouth) — a *member* speaks. 職's radical is 耳 (ear) — you *listen* at work. 求's radical is 氷 (water) — you *seek* water to survive.
